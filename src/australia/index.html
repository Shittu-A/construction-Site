The World Food Platform Australia Region

The World Food Platform Australia is a regional initiative designed to support one of the world's most export-oriented and innovation-driven food systems. Australia plays a critical role in global supplies of grains, meat, dairy, seafood, and high-quality agri-products, while operating in some of the world's most climate-challenging production conditions. Read more

Food systems in Australia are shaped by vast geography, water scarcity, climate extremes, and long-distance logistics. Droughts, floods, and rising input costs place constant pressure on producers, while access to global markets remains essential for economic viability. At the same time, Australia is a leader in agricultural research, biosecurity, precision farming, and sustainable land management. The World Food Platform Australia provides a structured framework to align these strengths with future needs.

The Platform connects governments, agribusinesses, farmer organizations, research institutions, technology providers, and investors. It supports collaboration across climate-resilient agriculture, water efficiency, food security, trade facilitation, innovation adoption, and biosecurity standards.

By linking Australia's expertise with global networks and markets, the World Food Platform Australia strengthens resilient, competitive, and future-ready food systems. It supports sustainable growth, protects natural resources, and contributes to long-term regional and global food security.
